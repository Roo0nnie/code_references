




Global demography, which is the number of individuals in the globe who are responsible 
for the movements that have an impact on society in both positive and negative ways, 
has been significantly impacted by globalization, and the two are intricately connected. 
Strong economy is not usually correlated with large populations. The most populous nations
in the world, such as China and India, do not have high income levels or low unemployment
rates in comparison to other nations, such as those on the European continent.
